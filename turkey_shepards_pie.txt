Ingredients:

1 1/4 lb ground turkey
2 strips bacon (chopped but uncooked) (optional)
1 onion (minced)
2 garlic cloves (minced)
1 1/2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
1 tbsp flour
Salt and pepper to taste
3 cups mixed frozen vegetables (1 bag)
4 cups mashed potatoes (~3 big potatoes, enough to cover the cast iron)
Oil

0. Chop potatoes, and pre heat oven to 400
1. Place the chopped potatoes (~3) in a small, deep pot, cover with water and bring to a boil, covered, over high heat. Salt the water and cook until tender, 12 to 15 minutes. Then mash 'em up.
2. Steam the frozen vegetables (1 bag) as described on the package.
3. Meanwhile, heat oil in the pan on medium heat. Add the bacon (2 strips cut) and cook until crisp, about 3 minutes, then add the turkey (1 1/4 lbs) and break it up with a wooden spoon. Season with salt and pepper and cook until browned, about 5 minutes
4. Push meat to the side and add the onion (1 minced) and garlic (2 cloves minced) to the center of the skillet. Season with salt and pepper and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes, then mix everything together. 
5. Stir in the worcestershire sauce (1 1/2 tbsp), flour (1 tbsp),  and cooked frozen vegetables.
6. Layer on the mashed potatoes
7. Bake in the oven for 15-25 minutes, until the potatoes start to get hardened.
8. Set oven to broil and broil for ~4 minutes, until the potatoes get a nice brown color.
