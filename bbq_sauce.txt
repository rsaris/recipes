1 cup ketchup
1/4 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup apple cider vinegar
1 tbsp sriracha
1 tbsp soy souce
2 1/2 tbsp worchestershire sauce
1 tsp salt
1 tsp crushed garlic

1. Mix it up and put it on the stove at 5 (medium).
2. Once it starts bubbling, lower it to 2.
3. Simmer, stirring every few minutes for ~25 minutes.
