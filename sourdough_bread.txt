The morning before:
 1. Take the starter out and let it warm up.
 2. Split into two sections of 50g (1/4 cup) and feed both with 50g (1/4 cup) water, 50g (1/2 cup) flour.
 
The evening before:
 1. Put one of the two starter sections back into the fridge for use later.
 2. With the other starter section, discard all but 50g (1/4 cup) and feed again with 50g (1/4 cup) water and 50g (1/2 cup) flour.
 
The morning of:
 1. Mix 50g starter, 350g water (at ~80F), 500g flour, and 1 1/2 tsp salt until everything is incorporated
 2. Knead until you can hold it up and it does not tear
 3. Let proof for ~3 hours
 4. Fold over going in a round and shape into a ball by pulling towards yourself until there is a nice skin on the dough.
 5. Sprinkle flour into a towel lined bowl and put dough into bowl seam side up
 6. Let proof for ~3 hours
 7. Prehead oven to 450
 8. Using parchment paper, flip the dough on the parchment paper and place it into the dutch oven.
 9. Cook with the lid on for ~25 minutes
 10. Cook with the lid off for ~25 minutes (until it has a nice hard crust and a good hollow knock)
 11. Cook on the rack for another ~10 minutes
 12. Let cool on a cooling rack for ~1 hour
